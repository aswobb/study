## 本课重点

这一课主要练习 `be` 动词，也就是 `am / is / are` 的基本用法。

```text
I am ...
You are ...
He / She / It is ...
We / They are ...
```

例如：

```text
I am busy.
You are nice.
He is a student.
She is happy.
It is small.
We are friends.
They are teachers.
```

注意：

- `I` 后面用 `am`。
- `you / we / they` 后面用 `are`。
- `he / she / it / this / that / 单数名词` 后面用 `is`。
- `these / those / 复数名词` 后面用 `are`。
- `This is ...` 的意思是“这是……”，你写的例句都属于 `is` 的用法。

## 句型

```text
主语 + be动词 + 名词.
主语 + be动词 + 形容词.
```

例如：

```text
I am a student.
He is a teacher.
They are friends.
I am tired.
She is happy.
We are busy.
```

## 常见缩写

```text
I am = I'm
you are = you're
he is = he's
she is = she's
it is = it's
we are = we're
they are = they're
```

注意：`it's` 表示 `it is`，不是“它的”。“它的”是 `its`。
